# Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
Customer churn is one of the most important business problems because losing customers directly affects revenue and growth. This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior to identify the major factors influencing customer retention and customer loss.

The project was built using Python, MySQL, and Power BI to perform data cleaning, analysis, and interactive dashboard creation. The main goal of this project is to help businesses understand customer behavior, improve retention strategies, and reduce churn.

---

## Problem Statement
Businesses often struggle to retain customers due to inactivity, low satisfaction, poor engagement, and lack of personalized offers. Identifying churn patterns can help organizations take preventive actions.

This project analyzes customer data to answer:
- Why are customers leaving?
- Which customer groups are more likely to churn?
- How does spending behavior affect churn?
- Does membership type impact churn?
- Which cities have higher churn rates?

---

## Objectives
The main objectives of this project are:

- To analyze customer churn rate
- To identify factors affecting churn
- To study customer inactivity behavior
- To compare churn across membership types
- To analyze customer spending patterns
- To evaluate satisfaction impact on churn
- To identify city-wise churn distribution
- To generate business recommendations for retention

## Key Insights

- Customers with low satisfaction scores have higher churn rates.
- Inactive customers are more likely to leave.
- Bronze and Silver membership customers show higher churn compared to Gold members.
- Customers without discounts tend to churn more.
- Certain cities like Chicago and Miami show higher churn rates.
- High-spending customers are generally more loyal.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are suggested:

- Improve customer engagement strategies
- Provide personalized discounts
- Focus on low-tier membership retention
- Improve customer support quality
- Create loyalty programs for inactive customers
- Increase customer satisfaction initiatives
